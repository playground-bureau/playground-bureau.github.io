We hope this one is obvious but, at Monkey Business, respect and consent is absolutely essential. We welcome guests of any gender, racial, ethnic, sexual, age, and class identity, and want everyone to be able to express themselves fully, feeling free to engage in creative, collaborative participation. We have a zero tolerance policy for any kind of harassment, and when you commit to buying a ticket, we trust that you understand and support this outlook. Similarly we do encourage you all to go bonkers, but know your limits, and ensure that your actions don’t negatively affect others.

The festival site is gorgeous, and we aim to keep it that way; for ourselves, and for the ones who follow. We’ll have an appropriate amount of bins on site, and everything that is waste must end up in them. This especially applies to cigarette butts. There are no excuses for this, it is a fire risk. Bring a tin or an ashtray and if you need one, we will provide one. The same applies to all types of open flame - candles, camping stoves, BBQ’s, fireworks - please leave them at home.

Let’s build a sustainably enjoyable event where the community energy is renewable and the environment is left better than when we arrived. Like an island, or a spaceship full of monkeys.
