HQ


Why We're Building This

In an era built for speed, for work, for endless scrolling and spending and scrolling again, we are choosing something else.

We are building HQ: a home for community in Barcelona.

A real space for gathering. For being human together — without an algorithm measuring it, without a feed monetizing it, without a brand packaging it.

Because the truth is: we are more connected than ever, and somehow lonelier than ever.

  • Globally, nearly 1 in 4 people feel very or fairly lonely — over a billion individuals. (Gallup, 2024)
  • In Spain, 20% of the population reports frequent loneliness, with 13.5% living with it for more than two years. (Soledades.es, 2023)
  • In Barcelona, young people (16-24) report higher loneliness rates (26.5%) than even seniors over 65. (Ajuntament de Barcelona)

This isn't a personal failing.

It's the logical result of a world designed to separate us, distract us, and monetize every spare moment of our attention.



The Problems We See

Too much scrolling

We scroll more than we speak. We doom-scroll, shop-scroll, rage-scroll — numbing ourselves while reaching for something we can't name. What gets lost is real presence, real connection, real time.

The Illusion of Connection

Social media promises us community and delivers only more isolation. We have more “friends” than ever, and fewer people to call when it matters. Visibility has replaced belonging.

Distraction

The systems we live in profit most when we are isolated, anxious, and endlessly “engaged.” Loneliness isn't an accident — it's a feature.

Progress at All Costs

The ideology of “more, faster, newer” tells us that being still, being together, being slow is a waste of time. But what if the real waste is letting another year pass without building anything real?



What HQ Is

HQ is a real, physical space for gathering — not mediated through screens, not for sale, not for spectacle.

It's for:

  • Dinners without an end time.
  • Conversations that stretch into the night.
  • Workshops, rituals, tiny raves, project labs.
  • Crying sessions. Potlucks. Strange experiments.
  • Building projects, dreams, and communities outside of algorithms and profit margins.

It will be messy. It will be slow.

It will be deeply human.

It will be run by the cultural association A.P.E.S., but shaped by everyone who shows up.

Why It Matters

We believe that creating spaces for collective care, creativity, and connection is not a luxury.

It's a necessity.


A space like HQ isn't a “nice to have” — it's a quiet act of resistance against a world that wants us isolated, overworked, and distracted.


HQ is not a product.

It's not optimized.

It's not scalable.


It's a seed.

A place to remember how to be with each other — in real time, in real rooms, with real laughter, real grief, real joy, real mess.
