About the Bureau

We're stoked to announce that we've snagged the silver medal for Web Design at the Paris Olympics. We sadly lost Gold to these guys, but let's be fair—we never had a chance.
We're a non-profit collective of event organizers, DJs, artists, story tellers, cooks, makers, eaters, and jokesters based in Barcelona. The Bureau itself is still fresh, but we all have lots of experience making DIY festivals in and out of Catalunya like this, this, and this.
At the Playground Bureau, we care about creating spaces where everyone can let loose, dance, dance, dance, laugh together, make genuine connections, and feel right at home. We believe in the power of play to bring people together and leave the world a little brighter than we found it. We're all bumped out about the global decline in social connections, and we're doing littles things we can to push back against that trend ✨.
Shout-out to our sister collective, No Concerns, based in in Lithuania!
