🚗 ACCESS 🚗

The festival is around an hour's drive from Barcelona and there will be plenty of on-site parking.

By public transport, you can take the R6 from Plaça Espanya to Igualada, then take the taxi from there (around 20 euros).

We are considering organizing a shuttle, but don't count on this yet—we'll keep you posted.

🥘 FOOD 🥘
Freshly made food will be provided six times during the festival by the Bureau's Department of Feeding Hungry Souls, made up of great friends of ours who also happen to be professional and hobbyist chefs. However you are still advised to bring some supplies with you, like light snacks, fruits and things to keep you going for breakfast, and while the kitchen is closed.
🥤 DRINKS 🥤

We're asking each of you to contribute your favorite bottle of alcohol, soft drink, or a creative combo of both to our communal bar. Don't worry, we'll also stock up on water, soft drinks, popular spirits, and some beers. Once we're all set up, drinks will be on the house — no checks, no charges, no need to carry coins! But that doesn't mean we endorse getting absolutely bananas-drunk. Please enjoy responsibly :)

The much-acclaimed Tea House will be back this year! Tea House 2.0 will host workshops during the day and turn into a cozy lounge by the night. If the dance floor is too much for you, you can always come here to relax and make new friends. We'll also have plenty of drinking water available at the bar and the Tea House.

🏕️ SLEEPING 🏕️

Please bring a tent! We have a couple areas where you can camp. The campsites aren't right next to the dancefloors, so you should get good sleep at night! Which also means: make noise at the party areas and respect the sleepers! Vans and vehicle camping are also welcomed — there is a limited capacity for camping vehicles so please email us and secure a space.

👘 OUTFITS 👘

There's gonna be one evening during the festival where we kindly oblige you to dress up in a certain theme. We'll make sure it'll be something you can choose to go all out with or lightly participate in (though the former is recommended). We won't demand anything bat-shit crazy or require you to buy anything new. This will be announced a few months prior to the event!

🏊 SWIMMING POOL 🏊

There's a swimming pool at the venue, so pack accordingly! It might get a bit chilly during the night, but the days will be warm enough for swimming during a sunny Catalan September.
