Shuttle Bus Confirmation

MONKEY BUSINESS 3

If you are reading this, then your shuttle bus ticket to Monkey Business 3 has been processed successfully. Find your bus ticket at the bottom of this email. 

IMPORTANT: This is not the event entry ticket. This bus ticket is not valid without the festival entry ticket, which you can get here.
1. Shuttle Details: Barcelona → Festival
  • Date: September 24th, 2026
  • Time: 16:45 boarding
  • The bus will leave at 17:00 and will not wait for delayed arrivals
2. Pick-Up Location

The exact pick-up point will be confirmed closer to the date via a follow-up email. Keep an eye on the WhatsApp Group for real-time updates on the day.

3. Arriving at the Festival

As mentioned in the ticket description, the bus will drive you from Barcelona to Sant Llorenç de la Muga, the closest village to the festival, as the bus can't drive further into dirt roads. This town is a 10-minute drive from the festival location (or 1h walking). Once you arrive at Sant Llorenç de la Muga, we will have cars doing back and forth ready to take you to the festival. There might be a small wait to fit everyone in cars (or a great opportunity to meet other participants).

4. Key Festival Info (Quick Recap)
  • Program: MONKEY BUSINESS 3
  • Dates: September 24th–27th, 2026
  • Gates Open: Thursday, September 24th at 14:00
  • Gates Close: Sunday, September 27th at 18:00
  • Location: Solana de Riambau: https://maps.app.goo.gl/uVfU7gHi14eYs3Tn7
