6. Food Provisions ❦
It's sometimes debated whether Monkey Business is a music/workshop festival with food, or a food festival with music/workshop. It's that important to us.
Freshly made food will be provided six times during the program by the Bureau's Department of Deluctable Meals, made up of great friends of ours who also happen to be professional and hobbyist chefs. There will also be light breakfast every morning. Please be careful if you are allergic to love, as there will be a lot it in the food.
You are still advised to bring some supplies with you, like light snacks, fruits and things to keep you going.
